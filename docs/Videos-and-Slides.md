The latest deck — **Fusion: End-to-End Reactivity is a Silver Bullet** — is the best starting point. It explains how the problems Fusion tackles are connected and how Fusion addresses their root cause, then walks through a range of C# examples — from simple to advanced — drawn from the [Voxt](https://voxt.ai/) codebase.

The two earlier decks are kept for historical reference. They predate `ActualLab.Rpc` (back then Fusion clients talked to the server over HTTP), but all the concepts they cover are still intact.
